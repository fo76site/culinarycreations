Cozy Sheet-Pan Herb Chicken

Ready in 40 min Serves 4
weeknight sheet pan comfort

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken thighs
  • 1 lb baby potatoes, halved
  • 2 cups sliced carrots
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp dried thyme
  • Salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a sheet pan with parchment.
  2. Toss potatoes and carrots with 2 tbsp olive oil, garlic, thyme, salt, and pepper.
  3. Place chicken thighs on top, drizzle with remaining oil and seasoning.
  4. Roast 30–40 minutes until chicken is golden and cooked through.
  5. Finish with a squeeze of lemon, if you like.

Tip: Great with a simple green salad or steamed green beans.

Dessert

Five-Minute Mug Brownie

Ready in 5 min Serves 1
quick chocolate microwave

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 4 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 3 tbsp milk
  • 2 tbsp neutral oil
  • Pinch of salt
  • Chocolate chips (optional)

Steps

  1. In a microwave-safe mug, whisk dry ingredients.
  2. Stir in milk and oil until smooth. Fold in chocolate chips.
  3. Microwave 60–90 seconds until just set on top.
  4. Let cool slightly; top with ice cream if desired.

Swap milk for oat milk and use dark chocolate to make it extra rich.
